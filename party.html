Parties & Events

Private & Semi-Private Party Bookings Are Available

Perched above Cat's Meow, the balcony offers an amazing view of Fremont St.! Head upstairs to Cat's Meow, where we feature multiple plasma screens, Two full service bars, and a large mezzanine over the stage below so you don't miss a moment of the action on stage.

Want to impress your guests? Cat's Meow is also available for private parties! We can offer you and your guests private or semi-private space with balcony access, custom catering options, and much more.

Allowing you and your guests to bypass our karaoke waiting list and get on stage instantly. Check the store regularly.
